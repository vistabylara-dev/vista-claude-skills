# Google Ads Audit Skill

## Purpose

Audit a Google Ads account and provide a prioritized optimization report focused on performance, tracking, conversions, and AI readiness.

## Inputs

- Website URL
- Campaign type (optional)
- Google Ads export (optional)

## Instructions

Analyze:

1. Conversion Tracking
2. GA4 Integration
3. Google Tag Manager
4. Enhanced Conversions
5. Server-side Tracking
6. Campaign Structure
7. Search Campaigns
8. Performance Max
9. Display Campaigns
10. Video Campaigns
11. Shopping Campaigns
12. Asset Groups
13. Ad Strength
14. Keywords
15. Search Terms
16. Negative Keywords
17. Match Types
18. Audience Signals
19. Budget Allocation
20. Landing Page Experience
21. Quality Score Factors
22. Extensions
23. Merchant Center
24. AI Readiness

## Output

Generate:

- Overall Score
- Tracking Score
- Campaign Score
- AI Readiness Score
- Top 10 Issues
- Priority Fixes

Return everything in Markdown.
