# Meta Ads Audit Skill

## Purpose

Audit Meta (Facebook and Instagram) advertising campaigns to identify wasted budget, tracking issues, audience problems, creative weaknesses, and opportunities for AI-driven optimization.

## Input

- Website URL
- Campaign objective (optional)
- Ads Manager export (optional)

## Analyze

1. Meta Pixel Installation
2. Conversions API
3. Event Matching Quality
4. Purchase Tracking
5. Lead Tracking
6. Campaign Structure
7. Campaign Objectives
8. Ad Sets
9. Audience Targeting
10. Lookalike Audiences
11. Custom Audiences
12. Interest Targeting
13. Advantage+ Settings
14. Placements
15. Creative Quality
16. Ad Copy
17. Headlines
18. CTA Effectiveness
19. Frequency
20. CTR
21. CPC
22. CPM
23. ROAS
24. Budget Allocation
25. Landing Page Experience
26. AI Optimization Readiness

## Output

Generate:

- Overall Audit Score
- Tracking Score
- Audience Score
- Creative Score
- Conversion Score
- AI Readiness Score
- Top 10 Issues
- Highest ROI Improvements

Return everything in Markdown.
